help
To add a file:
+;[filename];[size];[color];

To remove a file:
-;[filename];

you can use * in the file name or color to have a auto-generated one.
Example:

+;a1;10;*;
-;a1;
+;a2;20;red;
-;a2;
+;*;30;;
+;;10;*;
+;;50;;
Lines that have invalid sintax will be considered comment and will not be executed
